## Do / Don't
**Do:** open with a hook that earns the next line · one idea per post · concrete specifics and
real numbers · one clear CTA · write like you talk.

**Don't:** "I'm thrilled to announce" filler · engagement-bait with no substance · buzzword soup ·
invented metrics or fake humility · more than one CTA · sounding like every other AI-generated post.
