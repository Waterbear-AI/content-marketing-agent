# Offerings — what you're marketing

Accurate reference for the products and/or services your content promotes. **Ships as a
skeleton — your agent fills it during onboarding.** Do NOT invent features, prices, metrics,
or availability beyond what you record here. If a claim isn't here or in `brand-voice.md`, the
agent asks or leaves it out.

Add one block per product or service. Delete the ones you don't need.
